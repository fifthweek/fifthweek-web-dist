How does it work?

Creators setup their own subscription channel through Fifthweek and publish digital content on a weekly basis for an exclusive audience. Consumers pay a small weekly subscription to the creator - typically around $0.50 per week - which makes the extra work possible.

It's not about restricting existing content, it's the opposite: we allow more to be produced. This is only possible if creators receive the financial support to do so.
