What is Fifthweek?

Fifthweek is a micro-subscription platform that makes it easy to create low-cost subscriptions that are convenient for subscribers.

We make it really simple to share great content with those who value it. It's like Tumblr, but for content worth paying for.

How it works

Creators setup their own subscription channel through Fifthweek and publish digital content on a regular basis. Subscribers pay a small weekly subscription to the creator, and receive more content in return.

It's not about restricting existing content, it's the opposite: we allow more to be produced.
